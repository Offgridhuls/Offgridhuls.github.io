Terminal Scape (Prototype)

Top-down, 2d, rogue-lite rpg made in Unity. The player is tasked with breaking into a network and battling security systems in order to steal data. The network is represented by a series of rooms with several terminals, interacting with which will start a combat event. The combat event is a turn based battle where the player and the enemy take turns playing abilities against each other until one party's Health Points reach 0. Upon victory, the player is awarded with a preset "data package" which can contain either an ability, ability upgrade, or a stat upgrade, and allow entrance into the next room. Entering the next room will increase enemy health and bandwidth. Upon player death, their stats and ability stack, as well as enemy difficulty are reset to the last save point.

Space-Capitalist (Prototype)

Your goal as Geoff Bozos, is to get a foothold on a new planet to pave the way into a developers fortune! If you let the inhabitants rob you of your money, you will find yourself escaping with nothing but the shirt on your back. The more trouble the inhabitants give you the less investors will believe in your idea. Once your out of money your development is over. Survive in this top down tower defense game, by gathering resources and building a path lined with defenses made from them. Displace all of the inhabitants to turn this planet into a money making machine.

Rise Of The Elements

Rise of the Elements is a real-time third person open-world action RPG that focuses on fantasy elements. In ROTE, the player has the opportunity to wield the powers of the elements of fire, water, earth, and air. Each element is manifested in the form of a unique world, and it will be the player's goal to travel across each of these worlds in search of stones that each harness the powers of the four elements. Each stone is protected by a boss, and collecting the stone symbolises respect for the land you've traversed, unlocking a path to a cure for the Rot.
